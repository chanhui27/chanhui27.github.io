Degree Program Mapper Project - Mobile Development

DPM Project

Degree Program Mapper is a mobile app designed for those who want to go to Wintec's IT and students in school. This app is for those who are first in Wintec IT and want to know about the Wintec IT department. For example, they can know four different majors in Wintec IT and different kinds of modules in each major. Moreover, this app will tell which modules are compulsory to take and which are not. Furthermore, for the students who are currently studying in Wintec, they can see the status of the major that they have chosen or see the modules they have taken, the ones they have been taking, and the ones they will need to take later. This app also allows a manager to manage it. For instance, the manager can edit the modules such as adding, modifying and deleting. This app was created through a team project.
